\section{Structure of polytope}

The polytope is defined by two pairs of a matrix and and a vector. $F$ being the vector of all flows at all timesteps, first one $(A,b)$ is an equality $ A.F = b$, second one $(C,v)$ is an eqality  $ C.F \le v$. For the Barents sea, we have: :

\begin{Schunk}
\begin{Sinput}
> dim(POLYTOPE$A)
\end{Sinput}
\begin{Soutput}
[1] 2593  775
\end{Soutput}
\begin{Sinput}
> length(POLYTOPE$b)
\end{Sinput}
\begin{Soutput}
[1] 2593
\end{Soutput}
\begin{Sinput}
> dim(POLYTOPE$C)
\end{Sinput}
\begin{Soutput}
[1]  64 775
\end{Soutput}
\begin{Sinput}
> length(POLYTOPE$v)
\end{Sinput}
\begin{Soutput}
[1] 64
\end{Soutput}
\end{Schunk}
